# General FAQs

## What does Vael Creative do?

Vael Creative is a full-service branding and web design agency. We help businesses build memorable brand identities and effective digital presences. Our services include logo design, brand strategy, website design and development, and digital marketing.

## Where is Vael Creative located?

We're based in the United States and work with clients across the country and internationally. We conduct most of our work remotely, making it easy to collaborate regardless of location.

## How long has Vael Creative been in business?

Vael Creative brings years of combined experience in branding, design, and digital strategy. Our team has worked with businesses of all sizes, from startups to established enterprises.

## What makes Vael Creative different from other agencies?

We take a strategic, thoughtful approach to every project. Rather than rushing to deliverables, we invest time in understanding your business, your audience, and your goals. This results in work that's not just beautiful, but effective.

## Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups because we get to help shape brands from the ground up. Whether you're pre-launch or looking to rebrand, we can help you create a strong foundation for growth.

## Can you help with just a logo, or do I need a full rebrand?

We're flexible. If you only need a logo, we can help with that. However, we often find that the best results come from a more comprehensive approach that considers your entire brand identity.
