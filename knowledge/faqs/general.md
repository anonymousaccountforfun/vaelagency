# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Vael Creative?

Vael Creative is a creative agency that produces premium, AI-accelerated content for consumer brands. We combine the efficiency of AI tools with human creative direction to deliver on-brand, high-quality, performance-ready work. We're human-curated and AI-accelerated, serving the boldest consumer brands.

## What is AI-accelerated creative?

AI-accelerated creative combines the efficiency of AI tools with human creative direction. We use cutting-edge technology to speed up production while experienced creative directors ensure every piece is on-brand, high-quality, and performance-ready. We lead with technology while centering on the human.

## What types of creative content do you produce?

We produce a full range of brand content including ad creative, brand photography, product photography, lifestyle photography, video production (short-form and long-form), social media content, UGC-style content, marketing copy, email design, logo design, sonic logos, sound design, graphic design, and web design.

## How fast can you deliver creative content?

Our turnaround times vary by project type:
- Paid Media Creative: 4-6 business days
- Launch Campaigns: 5-7 business days (2-3 weeks end-to-end)
- Seasonal Campaigns: 3-5 business days (1-2 weeks end-to-end)
- Brand Storytelling: 7-10 business days
- Average turnaround across all projects: 48 hours for standard deliverables

## How much does it cost?

All pricing is custom-scoped based on your specific project needs, brand goals, timeline, and deliverables. We don't use one-size-fits-all pricing. The best way to get an accurate quote is to book a discovery call where we'll discuss your goals and create a tailored proposal.

## Do you work with brands outside of New York?

Yes! While we're based in New York, we work with brands nationwide across the United States. Our AI-accelerated workflow allows us to collaborate effectively with clients anywhere.

## Who founded Vael Creative?

Vael Creative was founded in 2024 by Brian Hughes (CEO) and Chris McDonald (Chief Creative Officer). Brian is a former General Manager at Hims & Hers who previously led strategy at Spotify and operations at Uber. Chris is an audio engineer turned creative strategist who scaled sales at Epidemic Sound, Artlist, and UnitedMasters. The founders come from Uber, Spotify, Hims & Hers, Epidemic Sound, Artlist, and United Masters.
